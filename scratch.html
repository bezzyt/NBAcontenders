There are 30 teams in the NBA but (if we're going to be honest) not all of them are good. Only a few teams really have a chance to win the NBA Championship each year. These teams are called 'contenders'. So what makes a contender? Lets break it down:

To be a TRUE championship contender, an NBA team must have:

  1. 1 Superstar
    • Examples: Nikola Jokic, Steph Curry, Giannis Antetekounpo, LeBron James
    • This is the kind of guy that you can rely on at any given moment. They are the cornerstone of your team, and the ball will be in their hands for every clutch possesion.
  2. An Experienced Co-Star Complement
    • Examples: Jamal Murray, Klay Thompson, Khris Middleton, Kyrie Irving
    • When you find a player that is talented enough to be called a star but also perfectly complements the playstyle of your franchise player, you have caught lightning in a bottle. Duos like the ones above are historically hard to beat.
  3. A Veteran Supporting Cast
    • Examples: 2023 NBA Champion Denver Nuggets: Kentavious Caldwell-Pope, Aaron Gordon, Michael Porter Jr., Bruce Brown, Reggie Jackson, Jeff Green
    • The last ingredient: veterans who will not disappear nor crack nor choke in the playoffs.

    This begs the question: who are the true contenders for the 2025 NBA Championship?
